Michael Robson

My lighting techniques and design are considered cutting edge in the industry. My friendly Australian demeanor seems to make models and non-professional subjects alike feel very comfortable in front of the camera.

Background

A native of North Eastern Australia, I am one of those photographers who can make any shot work. I have a perfectionist when it comes to getting the shot right. I have been known to build complicated sets, brave all kinds of weather, and tether himself hundreds of feet off the ground in order to get the perfect photograph. As a former professional athlete I am very comfortable doing what it takes to get the job done.

Besides working all over the United States, I have worked in Scotland, Malysia, Tahiti, Australia, Thailand and the European Continent. I have worked in fashion, sport, outdoor, publishing, editorial and myriad other industries.

Skills

I am fluent with 35 mm, large and medium format in film or digital.
